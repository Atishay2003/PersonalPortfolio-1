# Pitch Notes

## 60-second pitch

Our project solves one of the biggest urban mobility problems: overcrowded buses and unreliable public transport schedules. We built a data-driven platform that predicts passenger demand using time, route, weather, and event conditions, then automatically recommends how to redistribute buses across the city. Instead of buying more vehicles, the city can use the same fleet more intelligently, reduce waiting time, and improve commuter satisfaction.

## Key innovation points

- AI-based demand forecasting for each route and time slot
- Fleet reallocation under a fixed bus limit
- Decision-ready output for transport authorities
- Easy future integration with live GPS and ticketing systems

## Questions judges may ask

### Where does the data come from?

For the prototype, we use realistic synthetic city data. In the real world, it can come from ticketing logs, GPS devices, IoT passenger counters, and mobile travel apps.

### Why is this better than a normal timetable?

A static timetable cannot react to demand spikes. Our system changes recommendations by scenario, such as rainy evenings, weekends, or event days.

### What is the business impact?

It reduces overcrowding, improves punctuality, uses the existing fleet better, and supports smarter city planning.
